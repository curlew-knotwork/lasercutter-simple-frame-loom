PROJECT: OPEN SOURCE LOOM ASSEMBLY
SECTION: MAIN FRAME AND STAND

COMPONENTS:
- Left/Right Side Rails (2x)
- Top/Bottom Crossbars (2x)
- Stand Legs (2x)
- Support Braces (2x)
- M6 Bolts and Wing Nuts (8x)

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. LAYOUT: Place the four frame rails on a flat surface to form a rectangle. 
2. CORNER JOINTS: Align the pre-drilled holes at the corners. Insert bolts from the outside facing in.
3. TENSIONING: Secure with wing nuts. Ensure the frame is "square" by measuring diagonally from corner to corner (measurements should be equal).
4. STAND ATTACHMENT: Position the stand legs at the midpoint of the side rails. 
5. ANGLE ADJUSTMENT: Attach the support braces between the legs and the side rails. Use the secondary hole sets to adjust the tilt angle for ergonomic weaving.
6. FINAL CHECK: Hand-tighten all wing nuts. The frame should not wobble when placed on a table.

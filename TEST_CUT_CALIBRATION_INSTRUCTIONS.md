PROJECT: PRE-CUT CALIBRATION
SECTION: THICKNESS CHECK

PURPOSE: To ensure your laser-cut or CNC parts fit tightly (kerf compensation).

PROCEDURE:
1. TEST STRIP: Cut a 50mm x 50mm square from your chosen material (e.g., 6mm Plywood or 3D Print filament).
2. CALIPER MEASUREMENT: Use digital calipers to measure the actual thickness at three different points. 
   *Note: "6mm" plywood is often actually 5.8mm or 6.2mm.*
3. SLOT TEST: Cut a small "comb" pattern with slots varying by 0.1mm increments (e.g., 5.8mm, 5.9mm, 6.0mm, 6.1mm, 6.2mm).
4. FRICTION FIT: Find the slot where the material piece slides in with firm pressure but does not fall out.
5. FILE ADJUSTMENT: Update your CAD/SVG "Slot Width" parameter to match this successful measurement before cutting the full loom project.
